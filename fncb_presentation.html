Key Insights

Revenue risk

17 of 20 branches are missing their monthly target

The entire network is operating below plan. London City holds the largest gap at $726K despite leading in transaction volume. Manhattan Main follows with a $450K shortfall against its $500K target.

Engagement gap

166 customers have never made a single transaction

Nearly half the customer base has no activity on record. These dormant relationships represent both an engagement opportunity and a data integrity risk worth investigating further.

Default risk

Personal loans carry the highest default rate at 11.1%

Business and mortgage loans show zero defaults. Personal loans carry significantly higher risk and the highest average interest rate at 9.1%, suggesting a need for stronger underwriting criteria.

Loan leadership

Los Angeles Downtown leads all branches in loan revenue

At $454K in collected loan revenue, Los Angeles Downtown outperforms every other branch in the network. Business loans account for the largest share of the portfolio at 60 loans issued.

Portfolio health

89% of all loans are currently active

The active loan rate sits at 89.35% with only 2.96% in default. The average interest rate across the full portfolio is 5.60%, and total outstanding balance stands at $25.83M.

Customer value

80% of customers fall in the mid value segment

The majority of the customer base sits in the mid value tier. High value customers represent 18.54% of the base and carry an average balance of $40.33K across all account types.
